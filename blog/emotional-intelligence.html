What is Emotional Damage Intelligence?

How is EQ Different to IQ?

Off the top of my head they are both as meaningless as each other, which is to say not totally but some people place a too great importance on a number than is actually warranted.

Why is EQ important?

Keeping composed makes a situation better for all those involved. Empathy makes it easier for others to speak.
